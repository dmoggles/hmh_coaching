GK

Goalkeeper

Last defender · First passer
Your main job
Stop shots - and when you have the ball, keep it safe. Never give it away in a dangerous area.
Do this
  • ✓Look up and scan the whole pitch - then choose the best place to pass to
  • ✓Play the safe ball that keeps us attacking - not the risky one near our goal
  • ✓Come off your line for balls played in behind
  • ✓Talk - tell your defenders what's coming
Not this
  • ✗Pass short into the middle of the pitch - if it's intercepted, they're through on goal
  • ✗Force a clever pass when you're not sure - when in doubt, kick it long
  • ✗Hold the ball under pressure - play simple and fast
  • ✗Go to ground too early in a 1v1 - stay big and wait
Distribution - read it, then choose
CB wide and free
Roll or throw to her feet. We build from the back and keep the ball.
Wide mid is open
Throw or kick into the wide channel - starts our attack quickly.
Striker has space - go long
Their defence pushed up? Hit her early and direct and get her through on goal.
Never do this
Pass through the middle. One interception = a goal against.

Read the picture, then pick the ball that keeps us safe and keeps us attacking.

CB

Centre Back

Defend · Stay behind the ball
Your main job
Keep them from scoring. Stay between the attacker and the goal
Do this
  • ✓Stay goalside - always keep yourself between the attacker and the goal
  • ✓Show the attacker away from goal and into wide areas
  • ✓If you get beaten, sprint back on the inside - don't chase from behind
  • ✓Use the middle midfielder as your pass when you're under pressure
  • ✓Talk to your partner - one presses, one holds back
Not this
  • ✗Dive in - hold your position and wait for the right moment
  • ✗Both push up at the same time
  • ✗Chase the ball wide - hold your central position
  • ✗Get dragged out wide when recovering - protect the middle first
  • ✗Force risky passes into the middle when you're under pressure

One of you presses. The other one covers behind. Always.

MID

Middle Midfielder

The link · Always available
Your main job
Stay in the middle so your teammates always have somewhere to pass
Do this
  • ✓Stay central - be the safe option
  • ✓Show for the ball when the defenders have it
  • ✓Win the ball back quickly when we lose it
  • ✓When the defenders pass to you, find the striker or a wide mid
  • ✓When they attack, sit in front of your defenders and block passes forward.
Not this
  • ✗Follow the wide players forward
  • ✗Drift wide and leave the middle empty
  • ✗Relax when we're on top - keep your shape
  • ✗Always play backwards just because it's safe

You're the safe pass. Always be somewhere a teammate can find you.

WM

Wide Midfielder

Attack wide · Sprint back
Your main job
Get forward and cause problems - then sprint back the moment we lose the ball
Do this
  • ✓Push wide and high when we have the ball
  • ✓Run into the box and arrive late
  • ✓Sprint back as soon as we lose it
  • ✓Got space out wide? Take your defender on, cross it or shoot
  • ✓Tuck in to defend when the ball's on the far side
Not this
  • ✗Rush forward before the team's settled — wait for control, then attack
  • ✗Jog back - sprint back, every time
  • ✗Watch when the other team has it
  • ✗Drift inside when we've got the ball - stay wide

Your freedom to attack is earned by your work when we don't have the ball.

ST

Striker

Score · Lead the press
Your main job
Score goals - and be the first to chase their defenders when they have it
Do this
  • ✓Hold the ball up and wait for support
  • ✓Make runs in behind their defenders
  • ✓Chase their CB when they have the ball
  • ✓Shoot when you can, don't wait for the perfect chance
  • ✓Follow every shot in - rebounds are goals
Not this
  • ✗Drop too deep - stay up as a target
  • ✗Hold the ball too long in tight spaces
  • ✗Give up after the first chase - keep going
  • ✗Try to run through pressure - find a teammate in space

When you chase their defender, the whole team presses with you. You start it.
